# Powerbank Rental System - Roles and Sections

## Franchise Role

### Station Control Section
**Features:**
- Station detail with powerbank details
- Powerbank ejection

### Payout Section
**Features:**
- All past transaction history
- Available payout amount

### Add Sub Vendor Section
**Features:**
- Station or rental transaction details of assigned specific station

### Agreement Section
**Features:**
- Create agreement with sub vendor
- Show agreement detail
- Revenue model (fix price or certain percentage)

---

## Vendor Role

### Station Section
**Features:**
- Station detail (readonly)

### Payout Section
**Features:**
- All past transaction history
- Available payout amount

### Agreement Section
**Features:**
- Show agreement detail
